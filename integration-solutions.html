Requirements

The end client is a CPA firm, offering diverse accounting-based consulting services to customers as well as business.

 

In this digital era, one major challenge that most of the CPA firms face relates to sourcing and analyzing of data from multiple sources. With an increasing number of data sources, complexity in information, and increasing regulations with regards to data, there is a need to have a robust platform that will make this process of data generation/capture, storage, usage, and archival/disposal seamless and secure through automation and optimization.

 

The end client was facing a challenge wherein it needed access to aggregate financial information from various sources. So, a robust and secure solution needed to be developed to handle the data lifecycle and analysis process using MS Azure PAAS Solution. Integrating the solution with Yodlee, a third-party financial data aggregator, helped the client quickly and securely access critical customer data.

 

The high volume, velocity and variety of information assets require advanced forms of processing if they are to be used as insights for decision making. For CPA firms, access to information from multiple banks or financial institutions is the key to assessing their customers’ financial health and provide advisory or consulting services based on this data. Bank statements, information on assets & liabilities, transactions, investment holdings, and current account data make up a chunk of this data. Apart from a holistic view of their customer’s data, the CPA firm also needs regular access to the financial data to do further analysis of their customers' assets and liabilities. The client wanted a customized solution that will help them access the required data from Envestnet Yodlee, a data aggregator platform, subject to approval from their end customers.

 

SOLUTIONS OFFERED

 

• A customer portal was developed using Azure Webservice, which allows the customers of the end client to grant or revoke access to their data, sourced through Yodlee API. This builds trust and transparency to the client’s consulting services.

 

• It also enables the end customers to link or unlink their bank accounts in such a way that they have full control of granting access to select-accounts.

 

• The Internal Portal has functions to manage the customer onboarding, user-account set up and ondemand data retrieval.

 

• On-demand data retrieval includes a facility to share the downloaded data via Citrix Share, which eventually populates the data to the targeted server of the client.

 

• The portal data is stored on Azure SQL Database- an intelligent, scalable, relational database service built for the cloud.
