Data Driven Facial Recognition System For Spacial Temporal Data

Requirements

The main goal of this project will be to build a model that will eventually take a video of people in the wild (of a social event), a video in dynamic condition with not ideal quality and lighting. Process the video and create a database of the people observed and recognized with definite certainty, record the data of each encounter in the form of time and duration on a list, in addition to images that display a variety of conditions (quality, scale, lighting etc).

 

1- build a custom model to process a video and perform analysis for prediction of spacial-temporal data of where a given face/person was seen throughout a video at different times.

 

2- to reconstruct path of person detected/recognized on the local spacial-temporal space, and track the spacial movement before and after given position in space-time and output first/last detected timestamp of encounter.

 

3- Organize the data in way that optimize the comfort of use.
